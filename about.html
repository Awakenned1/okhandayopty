About Okhandayo PTY LTD

Building a better future through sustainable development and community empowerment.

Our Story

Okhandayo PTY LTD was established in 2013 as a 100% black-owned company with a vision to create more jobs for unemployed South Africans, uplift the standard of life through social intervention, and develop communities to be more self-reliant.

Our founder took the opportunity offered by South Africa's democracy to become a job creator rather than a job seeker. We aim to participate in the mainstream of socio-economic development by addressing social challenges and creating job opportunities for youth, unemployed men, and women.

Our Core Values

Our Mission and Vision

Our Mission

To provide quality services to our clients, create sustainable jobs for the unemployed and disadvantaged communities, and participate in addressing the government's vision of infrastructure development.

Our Vision

To become a highly recognized company that provides quality work in both social and construction fields for our clients and the communities we work with.
